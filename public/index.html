About DJ Latina

Hi, I'm Alicia, AKA DJ Latina.

I'm a 26-year-old full-time Mexican-American female DJ based in San Diego, CA. I get booked to DJ weddings, corporate banquets, parades, music festivals, county fairs, and entertainment venues all over Southern California. I am a native Spanish speaker and am known for rocking out dancefloors all over SoCal to both English and Latin music.

I play all genres of music including but not limited to top 40, hip-hop/R&B, Latin, oldies, and funk/disco. I'm very accomodating to specific requests and serve all races, ethnicities, age groups, cultures, and lifestyles.

I'm 100% invested in making your event. I have top-of-the-line equipment, formal training from a professional DJ academy, and am trained to prevent and intervene in the event of a technical malfunction.
