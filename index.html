DJ Latina of Experiencia Latina Productions

Experiencia Latina Productions LLC is a San Diego-based company in the entertainment industry operated by DJ Latina that proivdes bilingual DJ/MC, event production, AV design & installation, live sound technician, and design & branding services.

TOP-OF-THE-LINE EQUIPMENT

Experiencia Latina Productions provides top-of-the-line brand-name sound reinforcement, intelligent lighting, wireless microphone, projection-mapping, and DJ mixing equipment worth almost $100,000.

TECHNICAL EXPERTISE

DJ Latina has a professional and educational background in engineering. She also has formal training in mixing from a professional DJ academy. In addition, she has completed courses in audio visual design, event production, 3D modeling, and electronics.

END-TO-END PLANNING

Experiencia Latina Productions puts the client's vision, needs, and preferences at the center of the planning stage. A 3D model of your production package will be provided so the client can visualize the full audio, lighting, and video experience.

UNFORGETTABLE EXPERIENCE

DJ Latina's live music mixing on turntables, captiviating MC skills, powerful dance floor lighting, and nightclub-style fog and effects make for an unforgettable experience for your event.

FAQ

How far advance do I have to book DJ Latina?

Whether your event is two years or two days down the line, try giving DJ Latina a call. If your date is open on her calendar, she'll happily take it on.

What is the process to book DJ Latina?

DJ Latina offers a free over-the-phone or in-person consultation to discuss your event details, event vision, preferences, and audio-visual needs. She is very accomodating to and willing to discuss specific requests.
For large projects and events, she visits the venue in person.
If required by the venue, DJ Latina does have liability insurance and can provide them with a certificate.

Does DJ Latina charge for setup and breakdown?

No. Never. No matter how long it takes, you'll never have setup and breakdown time added to your bill.
